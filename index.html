Weakly Saturated Submonoids
& the Twin Prime Conjecture

A Furstenberg-style proof that the submonoid $N=\\langle G\\rangle\\leqslant(\\mathbb{Z}^2,\\otimes)$ has infinitely many irreducibles, via the coset identity $\\widehat{q}\\otimes N = N\\cap(\\widehat{q}\\otimes M)$ and the subspace topology from $M$. Formalised in Lean 4.

$$\\widehat{q}\\otimes N = N\\cap(\\widehat{q}\\otimes M)\\quad\\Rightarrow\\quad |\\mathrm{Irr}(N)|=\\infty\\quad\\Rightarrow\\quad\\text{infinitely many twin primes}$$

ABSTRACT
The Coset Topology Approach
$\widehat{q}\otimes N=N\cap(\widehat{q}\otimes M)$ is the key.
01
DEF 1.1
Operations $\oast$ and $\star$
$x\oast y=6xy+x+y$, $x\star y=-6xy+x+y$, id $0$.

THM 1.3
Isomorphisms $\phi,\psi$
$\phi(x)=6x+1:N^*\cong(6\mathbb{Z}+1,\cdot)$.
03
LEM 1.4
No Zero Divisors
$u,v\neq 0\Rightarrow u\oast v\neq 0$.
04
DEF 2.2
$G$ and $N=\langle G\rangle$
$G=\{(k,k)\}$. Elements of $N$: $(z,w)$ same sequence, different ops.
05
PROP 2.3
$M$ Topological Monoid
$\otimes$ continuous in Furstenberg product topology.
06
LEM 3.1
$\widehat{a}\otimes M$ is Clopen
$(a_1+(6a_1{+}1)\mathbb{Z})\times(a_2+(6a_2{-}1)\mathbb{Z})$.
07
THM 3.2
Coset Identity
$\widehat{q}\otimes N=N\cap(\widehat{q}\otimes M)$ for $\widehat{q}\in\mathrm{Irr}(N)$.
08
COR 3.3
$\widehat{q}\otimes N$ Clopen in $N$
Subspace topology gives clopenness for free.
09
THM 4.2
Irred $\iff$ Twin Primes
$(z,w)\in\mathrm{Irr}(N)\iff 6z\pm1\in\pm\mathbb{P}$.
10
LEM+COR 4.4–4.5
Norm Descent + Covering
$N\setminus\{0\}=\bigcup_{\widehat{q}\in\mathrm{Irr}(N)}\widehat{q}\otimes N$.
11
LEM 5.1
No Singleton Open in $N$
Every nonempty open set in $N$ is infinite.
12
THM 5.2
$|\mathrm{Irr}(N)|=\infty$
Furstenberg: finite $\mathrm{Irr}(N)\Rightarrow\{(0,0)\}$ open. Contradiction.
13
COR 5.3
Infinitely Many Twin Primes
$|\{k:6k\pm1\in\pm\mathbb{P}\}|=\infty$. $\blacksquare$
